Self-service is the promise at the heart of every internal developer platform — but delivering on it is harder than it looks. In this session from SUSE In Review, we explore how organisations can empower developers to move faster without sacrificing the guardrails that platform and ops teams depend on.

Covering the principles behind effective self-service platforms, common failure modes, and how SUSE's portfolio supports teams in building platforms their developers actually want to use.
