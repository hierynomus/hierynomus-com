The flagship demo showcase of SUSECON 2026. Demopalooza is SUSE's premier live demonstration event — a fast-paced, high-energy session where engineers and product experts take the stage to show (not just tell) what the SUSE portfolio can do.

This keynote brings together live demonstrations spanning the full SUSE stack: from SUSE Linux Enterprise and openSUSE at the foundation, through Rancher and NeuVector for Kubernetes management and security, to SUSE Virtualization for bridging the VM and container worlds.

No slides. No marketing fluff. Just live demos, real environments, and the occasional thing going unexpectedly right.
