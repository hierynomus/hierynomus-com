If you studied a bit about Kubernetes, you know this is complex and not that much secured by default. You probably also discovered that K8S itself is not enough: you need CNI, CSI, Ingress, load-balancer, certificates,... Etc etc...

When it comes to highly critical information systems, you have no choice but to take these topics seriously. In this session, we will introduce Airbus Defence and Space's approach to harden Cloud Native ecosystems in air-gapped environments for critical applications.
